Federal Bureau of Investigation
Crime Data Explorer

The FBI's Crime Data Explorer (CDE) is the FBI’s Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) Program's dynamic solution to presenting crime data in a more immediate venue that reflects the constant change in the nation’s crime circumstance.The CDE pages provide a view of estimated national and state data, reported agency-level crime statistics, and graphs of specific variables from the National Incident-Based Reporting System.The Crime Data Explorer is part of the FBI’s broader effort to modernize the reporting of national crime data. It allows you to view trends, download bulk data, and access the Crime Data API for reported crime at the national, state, and agency levels.
